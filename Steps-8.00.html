Step 3d: Key Findings


4 bedroom homes have an unusual amount of volatility in their prices. This could be driven by 1) having relatively fewer houses

of this type in the market at any give time, 2) a non-linear demand for this type of property, 3) or possibly unrealistic seller

expectations overpricing their property.

We also see that the more expensive 4 bedroom homes has an outweighed effect on the All Homes median price as the

movement of these two lines seem to mirror each other. An outweighed effect would be caused by the higher price of the larger

sized property or having more of this property type on the market relative to the 3 and 2 bedroom homes.

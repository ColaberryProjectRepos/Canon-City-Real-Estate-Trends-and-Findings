Finding Data Source

Project Goal: Utilizing internet  as a data source  to find and visualize the trend of Real Estate Market of Canon City, CO


Step 1 Finding Data Source

There are numerous data sources available online to get Real Estate data. We used quandl.com to get data through free API and Realtor.com to get CSVs.

About Quandl.com

Quandl.com is a great source of Data sets for multiple industries. It is used by professionals to analyze data in Hedge funds, asset managers and investment banks.

About Realtor.com

Realtor.com is a great source to buy, sell, lease or rent your property. But they are also generous with their data and allow certain data to be downloaded in CSV format.

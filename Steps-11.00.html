Step 4c: Key Finding


Canon City real estate prices have significantly outpaced consumer inflation. However, the US real estate market as a

whole also outpaced consumer inflation. Canon City demonstrated higher volatility than the US, which is to be expected

as it is a less diversified market.

Something significant happened in 2018 which drastically increased prices in Canon City up to the median for the US

as a whole. This finding calls for a more detailed and targeted analysis. As seen earlier, the 4 bedroom houses also

changed the median prices for Canon City. We may be seeing builders building larger houses which have put Canon City

on a new pricing trajectory. Or perhaps, we may be seeing an economic boom in the town that allows residents to afford

larger houses or an influx of new wealthier residents.
